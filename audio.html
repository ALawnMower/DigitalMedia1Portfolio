30 Second Audio | December 10, 2020

Students will use Audacity and primitive sounds to create a 30 second multi-track audio piece that either tells a story, or creates a music beat. Students should start thinking about UDMAF

Ringtone | January 5 2021

 Tired of hearing your same ring over and over again? Do you hate your Mom’s ringtone and wish she would change it up? Well, now is your chance to create your own! No copyrighted music! Ringtone must be between 5 and 20 seconds in length You must use at least 2 tracks in your song Have your ringtone slowly fade in, so your phone doesn’t make you jump when it starts ringing. Have fun and make it something you enjoy!
